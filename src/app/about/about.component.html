About Website:
The goal of Bilgi Chatbot website is to provide a platform to handle any form of question about Istanbul Bilgi University.
Users can:
  • ask questions to our chatbot
  • find their question from suggestions
  • check out frequently asked questions
  • directly send questions to Bilgi University staff
